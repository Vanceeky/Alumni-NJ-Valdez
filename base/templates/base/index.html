About

NJ Valdez Colleges Foundation (NJVCF) is a private, higher educational institution in Villasis, Pangasinan. It offers bachelor's degree courses in Information Technology, Teacher Education, Accountancy, and Business Administration. These programs are registered with the Commission on Higher Education (CHED), and students enrolled in these programs can now avail of financial assistance through the Unified Financial Assistance System for Tertiary Education Act (UNIFAST). Also available in the NJVCF are TESDA courses in Caregiving, Health Care Services, and Shielded Metal Arc Welding.
